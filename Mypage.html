Main key features

Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) is a well-known university in India offering various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral courses in engineering, technology, pharmacy, and management. Admission to JNTU is highly competitive, and students need to fulfill certain eligibility criteria and clear entrance exams to secure a seat.

The eligibility criteria for JNTU admission vary depending on the course and level of study. The basic eligibility criteria for undergraduate courses are:The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry as core subjects.The candidate must have secured a minimum of 45% marks (40% for reserved categories) in the qualifying examination.For postgraduate courses, the eligibility criteria are:The candidate must have completed a bachelor's degree in the relevant field with a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved categories).The candidate must have cleared the relevant entrance exam (GATE, APICET, etc). (GATE, APICET, etc.).

Entrance Exams JNTU conducts several entrance exams for admission to various courses. Some of the popular entrance exams are:Engineering, Agriculture and Medical Common Entrance Test (EAMCET) (EAMCET)Postgraduate Engineering Common Entrance Test (PGECET) \sIntegrated Common Entrance Test (ICET) \sEducation Common Entrance Test (EdCET) (EdCET)To be admitted to the course of their choice, applicants must perform well on these exams. Depending on the course and level of study, different exam formats and syllabuses are used.
